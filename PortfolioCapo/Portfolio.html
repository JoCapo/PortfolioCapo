App Design

Concepts for an app that focuses on household and couple organization. Mockups of the interface were made in AdobeXD for a class project in 2022. Shown are the opening page where you log in or create an account, the homepage where recent activity and upcoming events are shown, and the list page where the user can organize and share lists with others in the household.

Web Design

An early project I made from scratch to talk about myself and my work, as well as an updated version below. Made in VSC with raw HTML and CSS for a class project in 2020.

A lot of my personal projects are more graphic design focused and take inspiration from music or art pieces I like. Some projects shown are removing the background of older art pieces and giving them modern colorful backgrounds.
Another project I undertook was converting songs I like into VHS or poster designs. I enjoy layering effects to get old print aesthetic and working with typography elements.
